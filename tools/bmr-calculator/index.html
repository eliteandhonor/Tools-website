FAQ

What does BMR stand for?
BMR stands for Basal Metabolic Rate, the number of calories your body uses at rest.
Why should I know my BMR?
Understanding your BMR helps you plan calorie intake for weight management.
Can I use pounds and inches?
This version is metric only, but you can convert units with the Unit Converter tool.
Does it consider activity?
No, BMR measures resting calories. Use it with activity multipliers to plan diets.
Is this tool free?
Yes, the BMR Calculator is completely free with no ads or tracking.

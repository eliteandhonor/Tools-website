FAQ

What rates are used?
Rates are example values set in the script so the tool works offline.
How accurate are the conversions?
The conversions provide an approximation; for live rates you would need an online API.
Can I swap currencies easily?
Yes, simply switch the selections for From and To then click convert again.
Will you store my data?
No, the amounts you enter stay solely in your browser.
Is there a cost?
No, the Currency Converter is free to use and contains no ads.

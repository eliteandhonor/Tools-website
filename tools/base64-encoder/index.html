FAQ

Is there a size limit?
The tool is best for short strings but can handle a few kilobytes of text.
Can I decode images?
You can decode base64 strings representing small images, though they will display as text.
Is my data stored anywhere?
No, all encoding and decoding occurs in your browser only.
Does it require special permissions?
None at all. Just open the page and start encoding or decoding.
Will it work offline?
Yes, once the page is cached you can use it without internet access.

Stopwatch

Stopwatch lets you track elapsed time with precision. This online stopwatch starts and stops with a tap or spacebar press, then resets to zero on command. FAQ

Does the stopwatch keep counting when hidden?
It continues as long as your browser remains open and active.
Can I record lap times?
This basic version shows total time only, but you can pause and resume as needed.
Is the display accurate?
The timer uses JavaScript's clock with updates every 100ms for good precision.
Will it drain my battery?
Continuous timers use minimal resources but consider dimming your screen.
Is it mobile friendly?
Yes, buttons are sized for touch input so you can track time on the go.

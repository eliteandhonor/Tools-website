FAQ

What is Unix epoch?
It is the number of seconds that have elapsed since January 1, 1970 at 00:00:00 UTC.
Does it handle time zones?
The conversion uses your local time zone for display, so results reflect your computer settings.
Can I use milliseconds?
Enter full seconds only. You can multiply or divide by 1000 for milliseconds if needed.
Is the tool mobile friendly?
Yes, the responsive layout works well on phones and tablets.
Will it work offline?
After your first visit all scripts are cached so the converter functions offline.

FAQ

It follows the standard BMI formula, which is a general guideline rather than a medical diagnosis.

Currently, the calculator expects kilograms and centimeters for simplicity.
Is it mobile friendly?

Does it track my BMI history?

Will it work offline?
Absolutely. Once loaded, the calculator functions without an internet connection.

FAQ

What formula is used?
The tool applies the standard amortization formula to show the monthly payment.

This demo focuses on principal and interest only for clarity.
Will it show the total interest?
Yes, you'll see total interest and total paid.
Can I adjust the payment frequency?
No, it's currently fixed to monthly installments.
Is any personal information stored?
No, all calculations stay entirely in your browser.
